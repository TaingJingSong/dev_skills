## Required Sections (in order)

### 1. Instructions for Use
Bullet list of usage instructions:
- Record current issues in the Issue Log; potential future events in the Risk Log
- Update status, owner, due date, mitigation, and resolution regularly during project review meetings
- Use the scoring guide below to classify priority consistently

### 2. Priority / Scoring Guide
Table: Level | Issue Severity | Risk Impact / Probability | Guidance
Rows:
- 1 | Low | Low | Limited impact; monitor through routine follow-up
- 2 | Medium | Medium | Visible impact on scope, timeline, cost, or quality; action needed
- 3 | High | High | Major impact on delivery or customer acceptance; immediate action required
- 4 | Critical | Very High | Threatens project success, regulatory compliance, or go-live readiness
- 5 | Status | Status | Suggested values: Open, In Progress, Mitigated, Resolved, Closed, Accepted

### 3. Issue Log Summary
Summary totals table: Total Issues | Open | In Progress | Resolved | Closed | Escalated
(Single row of count values)

### 4. Detailed Issue Log
Capture active problems that have already occurred.
Table: ID | Date Raised | Issue Summary | Impact | Priority | Owner | Due Date | Status | Action Plan | Closed On
- IDs: I-001, I-002, I-003 …
- Priority: 1 (Low) / 2 (Medium) / 3 (High) / 4 (Critical)
- Status: Open / In Progress / Resolved / Closed / Escalated
- Minimum 5 blank / placeholder rows

### 5. Risk Log Summary
Summary totals table: Total Risks | Open | Mitigated | Accepted | Closed | Escalated
(Single row of count values)

### 6. Detailed Risk Register
Capture uncertain future events that could affect scope, timeline, cost, quality, security, compliance, or operations.
Table: ID | Risk Statement | Category | Prob. | Impact | Score | Mitigation | Contingency | Owner | Status | Target
- IDs: R-001, R-002, R-003 …
- Category: Schedule / Budget / Technical / Resource / Scope / Compliance / External
- Prob. + Impact scale: 1–4 (matches scoring guide)
- Score = Prob. × Impact (auto-calculated guideline)
- Minimum 5 blank / placeholder rows

### 7. Action Tracker / Follow-Up
Table: No. | Ref. ID | Action Item | Owner | Target Date | Progress % | Remarks
- Ref. ID links to I-XXX or R-XXX

### 8. Escalation & Review Record
Table: Review Item | Frequency / Trigger | Participants | Notes
Rows:
- Weekly review | [Weekly / Bi-weekly] | [PM / Team / Customer] | [Notes]
- Escalation trigger | [Critical / Overdue / Customer impact] | [Sponsor / Steering Committee] | [Notes]
- Last review date | [Date] | [Meeting name] | [Notes]

### 9. Approval / Sign-Off
Confirmation that the Issue & Risk Log has been reviewed and is the current controlled version for the stated reporting period.
Table: Name | Title / Role | Signature | Date (3+ rows)
